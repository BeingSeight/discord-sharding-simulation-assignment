If 50000 users join in 5 minutes and one channel gets 80 percent of the traffic the single server will crash really fast. First the memory will fill up cause we are just appending messages to an endless list globally. The cpu will get choked trying to handle thousands of connections at the exact same millisecond. Since 80 percent of traffic is hitting one channel that specific part of the system will become a huge bottleneck and lock up preventing even the quiet channels from working.
